I'm a final-year Computer Science (AI & ML) student building applications that turn messy, real-world input — a blood report, a symptom description, a vulnerable endpoint — into a clear, actionable read. Outside coursework, I build and ship: three end-to-end projects live on Vercel and Render, spanning healthcare diagnostics and automated security scanning.
